The Society for Technical Communication is a professional association dedicated to the advancement of technical communication. Technical communicators research and create information about technical processes or products directed to a targeted audience through various forms of media.

Join STC as a member today!

STCTC, the Twin Cities chapter of the Society for Technical Communication, provides local professionals and students with a forum for new techniques and technology, networking opportunities, classes, and seminars. The atmosphere is welcoming and members are generous with their time and expertise.
